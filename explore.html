What grading system do we use for our restaurants?

0

0.25

0.5

0.75

1

We use a custom-made 5-point grading scale for each of our restaurants
to distinguish their level of allergy. 0-0.25 means that nearly all, or anything worth eating,
at the restaurant has the allergen in some way. All of the points you see when you press
a button gave an allergen rating of 0.5 or higher.
